LongEval Tasks

The Longitudinal Evaluation lab at CLEF 2023 is dedicated to promote the study of information access computer systems along the time. The CLEF community knows that one feature of text corpora is their evolution. Such inherent evlution is classically not considered information access evaluations. More precisely, we are concerned here with two fundamental tasks: Classification and Retrieval. Our goal is to allow the exploration of the behaviour of systems when their data evolve.
